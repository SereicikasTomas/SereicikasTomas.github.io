Intro

CLOSE

As you may already know, my name is Tomas and I am a Web Developer. I come from a small country in Eastern Europe called Lithuania. Until my late teens, I have played baseball. Yes, baseball, it's not very popular in Lithuania so everyone's reaction was always the same when they asked what sports I play. And afterward they would ask "So you are the one who hits or throws?". Well... I was both, you throw and you hit. That's how it goes. But one after another came serious injuries and I had to quit. I started to spend more and more time at my computer, which turns out was not really a bad thing.

From the moment I produced "Hello World" on the screen of my first web page, I knew I was hooked into the world of web development. But web development has never been "just a job" for me, it has offered an engaging challenge to continually learn and improve my skills. What started with a simple "Hello world" has become a full-fledged passion that only gets more and more exciting as the years go by.
